    When she's not making lists, coming up with unusual word combinations, or looking at trees; Jenni's great love is hand drawn illustration. Coincidentally, Illustration twins well with listening to the radio and drinking coffee.

    While at work on personal pieces in her studio, she grows fond of her whimsical characters for their personalities and imperfections. As they grow on the page of her sketch book, she can complete them in the way that they have prescribed.
