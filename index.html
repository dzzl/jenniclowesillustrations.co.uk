    Hello! Thanks for clicking your way here.

    I’m an Illustrator based in the North West of England.

    I graduated in 2009 with a degree in Graphic Design and Illustration at De Montfort University, Leicester.

    Since graduating i’ve returned to the North where I can often be found drinking coffee and drawing. I also enjoy cooking, yoga and staring at trees.

    My work is hand drawn, often using coloured pencils or watercolors and I like to render using my Mac.

    I work closely with on projects such as Wedding Stationery, Family Portraits and Small Business logos. You can read some of my client’s testimonials here.
